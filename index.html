Background

Hi, I'm Rosemary, and I'd love to help you achieve your health, fitness and weight loss goals! At Inspiring Fitness our aim is to provide you with an outstanding Personal Training service at your home, outdoors or place of work. I work exclusively with women. Personal Training with me gets results, be it weight loss, improved fitness, or simply being fit for life.

Why I Chose To Be A Fitness Professional

Sport has been my passion for most of my life. As a working mum with 3 children I know only too well how busy and stressful life can be. I find that keeping fit and healthy can really help us cope with whatever life throws at us. I really love the opportunity to help women fit exercise into their lives and see for themselves the benefits that this brings. We all deserve to look and feel our best and what better way to spend my time than to help women achieve this
